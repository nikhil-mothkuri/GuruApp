# SakshamBharat Brand Lockup

## Brand name

- **SakshamBharat**
- Meaning: Empowered India
- Positioning: a digital platform for learning, selling skills, and selling products across India

## Tagline

- **Learn, Sell, Grow — India empowered**

## Apple-inspired design philosophy

- clarity: keep the logo simple, luminous, and instantly legible
- deference: let the mark and wordmark feel light, removing visual clutter
- depth: use subtle gradients, layered shapes, and soft shadows
- familiarity: combine modern minimalism with an inviting, human feel

## Logo concept

- Icon: a smooth triangular mark formed from three rounded nodes and a growing path
- Meaning: network of people, upward growth, and opportunity
- Wordmark: `SakshamBharat` using modern, clean typography
- Tagline appears below the wordmark in a calm, secondary tone

## Logo lockup

- Icon to the left of the wordmark
- Brand name on one or two lines depending on layout
- Tagline below in lighter text

Example layout:

```
[icon]  SakshamBharat
       Learn, Sell, Grow — India empowered
```

## Font style

- Primary: **Poppins** (SemiBold / Bold for the brand name)
- Secondary: **Mukta** (Regular / Medium for tagline and body copy)
- Use refined letter spacing and generous line height for headlines

## Visual style

- generous white space and calm compositions
- rounded corners, subtle glass-like surfaces, and soft layers
- minimal iconography with a focus on content and readability
- warm brand accent used sparingly for CTAs and key highlights

## Usage guidelines

- hero section: use a white surface with small saffron accent and a subtle shadow behind the icon
- app icon: use the simplified mark inside a rounded square with a light gradient background
- buttons: use saturated saffron for primary CTAs and indigo for secondary actions
- cards: crisp white cards with thin shadows and slight blur behind top layers
