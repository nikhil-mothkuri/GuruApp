# SakshamBharat UI Mockups

## Design approach

The UI follows Apple’s design philosophy:

- clarity: simple layouts, generous white space, high contrast text
- deference: content is the focus, minimal chrome
- depth: layering with translucent surfaces and subtle shadows
- consistency: rounded shapes, smooth edges, restrained color usage

## Screen 1: Discover Home

### Purpose

Welcomes learners and sellers with discovery cards, search, and fast access to top categories.

### Features

- large title: `Discover skills & products`
- prominent search bar with subtle background blur
- featured cards for top gurus and trending products
- circular category chips with gentle shadow
- bottom action row: `Be a Guru`, `Sell a Product`, `Find a Skill`

### Apple-inspired styling

- bright white surface with soft gray background
- large, legible typography using Poppins / Mukta
- smooth card corners with 24px radius
- translucent blur panel behind the search input

## Screen 2: Guru profile

### Purpose

Highlights a guru’s service, quick booking CTA, and peer reviews.

### Features

- hero card with profile photo, badge, and headline
- skill tags in a pill style
- pricing card and `Book session` button
- review strip with star rating and recent feedback
- clean secondary card for related products or packages

### Apple-inspired styling

- layered cards with subtle depth and blur
- soft gradients on CTA buttons
- clean spacing and consistent iconography
- generous padding around touch targets

## Screen 3: Seller dashboard

### Purpose

Empowers sellers to manage products, track earnings, and list new offerings.

### Features

- top summary cards: `Earnings`, `Orders`, `Live Listings`
- product list with thumbnail, title, price, and status
- add product floating CTA button
- quick insight panel for performance trends

### Apple-inspired styling

- crisp white cards with thin shadows
- accent color only on key actions and metrics
- minimal text hierarchy and clear data grouping
- comfortable 16px minimum tap areas

## Colors and typography

- primary brand palette with saffron, indigo, and green accents
- use a mostly neutral surface palette: white, off-white, light gray
- typography: Poppins Bold for headlines, Mukta Regular for body copy
- button and card accents: saffron `#F47F2F`, deep indigo `#1E3A8A`

## Interaction notes

- use gentle hover states and touch feedback
- favor smooth transitions between content sections
- keep navigation light: top tabs or bottom bar with 4 items
- avoid heavy ornamentation; let the product & skills shine
